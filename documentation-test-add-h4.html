Stories

Add a story to the tool

  • Log into the Rainbow Tool using your network credentials

  • Find the URL of the story you want to add

  • These have to be stories or blog posts published in Methode or WordPress.

  • Look for the box on the right-hand side labeled Create an article

  • Paste the URL into the Article URL field. Select a Section from the dropdown menu.

  • (You should choose the section that the article will likely go into in the app, but you can easily change your mind later.)

  • Hit Create Article . You'll be brought to an article view.

  • Congratulations! Your article is now in the tool.

  • If someone else (or even you, earlier) has already added this URL to the Rainbow Tool, you'll see an error message saying ‘Article already exists' and a link to Edit it. Click ‘Edit it', and you'll see the article view described in Step 5.

Rainbow Preview app

The Rainbow Preview app is used to preview our content internally before we send it out live to the public. Only the Rainbow editorial team will be able to see it. We'll send each section to the Preview app, check and make sure everything looks great, and then publish it to the real app.

You can access the Rainbow Preview app on HockeyApp. If you don't have HockeyApp yet, see the instructions here.

  • In HockeyApp, you'll now see Project Rainbow Preview. It has a Rainbow pattern icon to distinguish it from the ‘live' app.

  • Download Project Rainbow Preview to your device.

  • Go to Apps on your Kindle; tap and hold on Project Rainbow Preview and select ‘Add to Home.' Now, it should stay in your top carousel.

Workflow

  • At the start of each shift, create each section for that edition

  • Follow the usual procedure of adding articles to the section

  • Put the section on the preview app early on, so you have plenty of time to preview, tweak and make the articles look great.

  • All sections in the preview app update much more quickly than the real app. You should see any change you make in the tool within 2-5 minutes.

  • Once you believe a section is ready to be sent out live, ask for a read-behind or otherwise mark it as ready ***(let's chat about the best way to do this)***

  • Between 4:30 and 5:00, make the section live on the real app once everything has been signed off on. (You do this like you always have by selecting Publish next to the section name, which sends it to the Master Section list.)

Put a section on the preview app

  • In the Sections part of the tool, you'll see a button next to all sections that says Preview .

  • Press this button next to the section you'd like to add to the Preview app. In the pop-up confirmation box, select Preview again.

  • Your section will now be in the box titled Preview Sections on the right-hand side.

  • Your section will appear on the Rainbow Preview App within 2-5 minutes.
